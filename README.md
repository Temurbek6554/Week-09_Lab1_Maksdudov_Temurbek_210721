Image Restoration (denoising)
Gaussian noise increases brightness variation and spreads histogram.

Salt & Pepper noise creates spikes at 0 and 255.

Median filter effectively removes salt & pepper noise without blurring edges.

Mean filter reduces noise but causes image blurring.

Geometric and harmonic filters provide better smoothing for Gaussian noise.

Min filter removes salt noise while max filter removes pepper noise.

Larger window sizes improve noise removal but increase blurring.
